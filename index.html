Bosses


Boss showcase! First boss is the Gravemound. An ape looking amalgam consisting of tombstones, bones and anything else you'd find in a graveyard. The model was made by my brother Shane. I weight painted and added animations from online as well as the AI behavior. Second boss is the reaper. A skeletal warlock that throws magic at you, summons skeletons, raises bones from the ground as an AOE attack and fades into the abyss only to come back at you with his scythe. The skeletal assets were made by Shane as well, but I made and painted the cloak, by running a cloth simulation over the skeleton. I then painted the cloak and the skeleton, weight painted it and gave is some animations as you can see below as well as some custom ones.


Here are some animation retargets for the boss, the reaper. Once characters are rigged and weight painted we retarget them mostly using animations from Mixamo.

Chaos Physics!
Maybe this explosion was a little underwhelming, but this clip showcases basic chaos physics in Unreal Engine. Basically it takes a static mesh and breaks it up, and applies physics and colliders to the individual pieces. This clip is just a little test to clear areas in our mines map. Pretty cool nonetheless.
